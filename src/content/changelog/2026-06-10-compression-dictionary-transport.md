New **Performance** topic on [Compression Dictionary Transport](/spec/performance/compression-dictionary-transport/) (RFC 9842) — reusing a previously served response, or a dedicated dictionary, as a Brotli/Zstandard dictionary so updated assets compress to a fraction of their size. Pure progressive enhancement over ordinary compression; status: optional.
