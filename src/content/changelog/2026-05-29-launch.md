First public version: a platform-agnostic specification of what a good website does, spanning Foundations, SEO, Accessibility, Security, Well-Known URIs, Agent Readiness, Performance, Privacy, Resilience and Internationalisation. Every topic is tagged required, recommended, optional or avoid, and cited from primary sources. Available as HTML, Markdown, a [checklist](/checklist/), `llms.txt`, RSS, and an [MCP server](/mcp/).
